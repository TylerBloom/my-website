About

TavernBot is a Discord bot for facilating trades between Magic: the Gathering players. People can manage a buy and sell list that's associated with their Discord profile. TavernBot can also help you connect with people that are looking to buy/sell cards that your looking to sell/buy.

The name - often in fantasy settings, the tavern is a place of interpersonal commerence, trading and deals. TavernBot seeks to facilate this with MtG card trading.

Impetus

Aside from asking other players that you know if they have a card that you're looking for, there isn't a good service for facilating trades (in-person or remotely). TavernBot seeks to fill this gap by oftening three major services. People can manage a buylist, manage a sell list, and visually track a trade between each other.
